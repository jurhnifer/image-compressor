## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Drag and drop an image onto the upload area, or click to browse
3. Adjust compression settings:
   - **Quality**: Control the compression quality (0.1 to 1.0)
   - **Max Width**: Maximum width in pixels
   - **Max Height**: Maximum height in pixels
4. Click "Compress Image" to process
5. View the comparison and download your compressed image

## Privacy

All image processing happens locally in your browser. No images are uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
